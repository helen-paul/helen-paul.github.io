Venue

We would love all our family and friends to join us for the weekend and relax without the need to travel between locations. To this end we have chosen Achnagairn Estate to be the venue for our wedding.

Accommodation

please indicate on our RSVP form if you would like to stay with us on the estate; we will then contact you with details of your room booking and how to pay.

all rooms are priced at £125 per night, including breakfast. Most rooms are doubles or twins, but there are some triples available.

We have exclusive use of the estate for the nights of Friday 8th and Saturday 9th October. There are two primary forms of accommodation:

The castle

The castle will be the focal point for the celebration, hosting the wedding, meals and providing plenty space for relaxing and socialising.

The castle at Achnagairn Estate has 24 bedrooms.

The Manors

Within short walking distance of the castle we have access to 7 manors which provide the majority of the available accommodation.

The manors each have 5 or 6 bedrooms and their own large kitchen/dining area. Breakfast will be provided in the manors for Saturday. We will all eat breakfast together in the castle on Sunday.

Travel

Achnagairn Estate, Kirkhill, Inverness-shire, IV5 7PD

Check in at Achnagairn is from 4pm.
Unfortunately, earlier arrivals are not possible.

if you will arrive after 8pm, please let us know in advance so we can make arrangements.

Arrival by Car

For those arriving by car, satnav or Google Maps will get you to Achnagairn using the postcode. Google might try to take you down a dirt track but the main entrance is well signed; you cannot miss it!

Arrival by Train

The nearest mainline train station is in Inverness. From here we can arrange for you to be collected. Please let us know your expected arrival time in advance.

Arrival by Plane

The nearest airport is Inverness. From here we can arrange for you to be collected. Please let us know your expected arrival time in advance.

There are daily flights from several UK cities as well as Amsterdam. Unfortunately there are no direct flights from Berlin; we recommend booking a connecting flight via London or Amsterdam.

Things You Need To Know

We will update this section with questions, as they come in. We hope this will already provide answers to the most common questions.

by when do we need to RSVP?
please RSVP using the form by 30th july, 2021
what will the weather be like?
the highlands in autumn can be a mixed bag: low sun or, rain a-plenty. either way, don't expect it to get too hot. the average temperature in October is about 9c.
can we bring our kids?
we've made the difficult decision to not invite children to the wedding. hopefully this advance notice allows you to still join us.
where should I park?
each manor has its own parking and there is a dedicated car park for castle guests.
what should I wear?

the wedding day will be semi-formal, and kilts and traditional dress are encouraged, but we’re sure anything you want to wear will be just great. dress up in something you love!

the ceremony, meal and dancing will take place indoors, but if the weather allows we’d love to make use of the beautiful garden so it’s probably a good idea to bring something to keep warm and shoes suitable for grass.

manor guests should also be mindful of the short walk to/from the castle via a gravel path. if it rains this path may get a little muddy, so you might want to change into your fancy shoes once you get to the castle.

should I have lunch before the ceremony?
the ceremony starts at 1.00pm, and our afternoon meal will begin around 4.00pm, so we suggest that you have a big breakfast or a light lunch beforehand. We’ll provide some lunchy bites in the fridges at the castle and in the manors.
what if I’ve never been to a ceilidh before?

then you’re in for a treat! there will be plenty of well seasoned dancers around to show you the ropes, but the band’s ‘caller’ will also go through the steps before each dance. wear shoes you can skip and spin in, or just kick them off!

if your question isn’t answered here, please give us a call or send us an email!
