Venue

We would love all our family and friends to join us from Friday to Sunday and relax without the need to travel between locations. To this end we have chosen the Achnagairn Estate to be the venue for our wedding.

We have booked the entire estate for the nights of Friday 8th and Saturday 9th October. The estate has two primary forms of accommodation:

The castle

The castle will be the focal point for the entire celebration, hosting the wedding, meals and providing plenty space for relaxing and socialising.

The castle at Achnagairn Estate has 24 rooms and can accommodate up to 55 guests.

The Manors

Within short walking distance of the castle we have access to 7 manors which provide the majority of the available accommodation.

The manors each have 5 or 6 bedrooms and can accomodate up to 65 guests. Each manor has its own large kitchen/dining area. Breakfast will be provided in the manors for Saturday. We will all eat brekfast together in the castle on Sunday.

Schedule

The details provided here will change over time, so please check back ocassionally to find out more information about the wedding schedule.

Friday, 8th October

Friday is all about relaxing in front of the fireplace after the journey to the Highlands. Dinner will be provided in the castle. There'll be a wedding rehearsal somewhere in the mix, too.

Saturday, 9th October

Breakfast will be available for both castle and manor guests. If the weather is on our side, you will have plenty of time to explore the grounds; the main ceremony will take place around 13:00. After a few drinks, dinner will be served in the great hall. The day will end with a traditional ceilidh, or simply relaxing in front of a fire.

Sunday, 10th October

Breakfast will be served for everyone in the great hall of the castle. After that, we'll have time to say our goodbyes and hit the road.

Q & A

We will update this section with questions, as they come in. We hope this will already provide answers to the most common questions.

When is the RSVP deadline?
Invitations will be sent out around June, 2021. The RSVP date will be 31st July, 2021
Can I bring a date?
Your invitation will make it clear if you are welcome to bring a guest.
Are kids welcome?
We've made the difficult decision to not invite children to the wedding. Hopefully this advance notice allows you to still join us.
What will the weather be like?
The Highlands in Autumn can be a mixed bag: low sun or, rain a-plenty. Either way, don't expect it to get too hot.
Where should I park?
Each manor has its own parking and there is a dedicated car park for castle guests.
What kind of shoes should/shouldn't I wear?
Manor guests should be mindful of the short walk to/from the castle via a gravel path. If it rains this path will get a little muddy, so you might want a second pair of shoes you don't mind getting a bit dirty. There is plenty space at the castle for changing into your fancy shoes.
